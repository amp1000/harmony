# Privacy Policy for Harmony

**Last updated: June 14, 2026**

Harmony ("we," "us," or "our") provides a caring companion and family check-in application (the "App"). We take the privacy of our users, especially older adults and their families, very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using Harmony, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Who this policy covers

Harmony is used by two types of people:
- **The primary user** (often an older adult) who uses Harmony as a companion and checks in each day.
- **Family members** who connect to a primary user to see their wellbeing status.

This policy applies to both.

---

## 2. Information we collect

**Information you provide:**
- Name or preferred name
- Approximate age and general location (if you choose to share it)
- Family members, hobbies, daily routine, and similar details you tell the companion to personalize your experience
- Medication names and reminder times that you enter
- Trusted contact names and phone numbers that you enter
- Conversations you have with the companion

**Health and wellbeing information:**
Because Harmony helps with daily check-ins and medication reminders, we may process information related to your wellbeing, such as your daily check-in status, the mood you select, and medication reminders. We treat this information with extra care. We do not sell it, and we do not use it for advertising.

**Information created through use:**
- Daily check-in records (the time and the mood you select)
- Medication reminder activity
- A simple activity history used to show family members how you are doing
- Basic technical information needed to run the App, such as device type and app version

**Payment information:**
If you subscribe, payment is processed by our payment provider (Stripe) or by the Apple App Store. We do not see or store your full card number. Our payment provider handles that securely.

---

## 3. How we use your information

We use your information only to:
- Provide the companion experience and personalize conversations
- Deliver daily check-ins and medication reminders
- Show connected family members your wellbeing status
- Send an alert to your chosen family member if you miss a check-in
- Process your subscription and free trial
- Keep the App secure and working properly
- Respond to your support requests

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do **not** use your conversations or health information for advertising.

---

## 4. How information is shared

- **With family members you approve:** When you share your family code with someone, you are choosing to let them see your wellbeing status, check-ins, medication activity, and recent app activity. You can ask us to disconnect a family member at any time.
- **With service providers:** We use trusted companies to run the App, such as hosting (Vercel), our database and login provider (Supabase), our payment provider (Stripe), our voice provider (where enabled), and our messaging providers used to send missed check-in alerts (such as an SMS or email provider). These providers process information only to provide their service to us.
- **For legal reasons:** We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of a user.

We do not share your information with anyone else.

---

## 5. Conversations and artificial intelligence

Harmony's companion uses artificial intelligence to hold conversations. The text of conversations is sent to our AI provider to generate responses. We use this only to provide the companion experience. Please avoid sharing highly sensitive information, such as financial account numbers or passwords, in conversation.

---

## 6. Your choices and rights

You can:
- View and edit your profile, medications, and contacts in the App
- Disconnect a family member
- Turn voice features on or off
- Delete your data by using the reset option in the App, which clears your information from the device, or by contacting us to request deletion from our systems
- Cancel your subscription at any time

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights, such as the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

---

## 7. Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide the App. If you delete your account or ask us to delete your data, we will remove it from our active systems within a reasonable period, except where we must keep certain records for legal or payment reasons.

---

## 8. Children

Harmony is intended for adults. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

---

## 9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including secure connections and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

---

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you in the App.
